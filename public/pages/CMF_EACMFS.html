See Materialise at MECSPE

June 9-11, 2022

Booth G25 - Bologna Fiere - Bologna, Italy

Join us at this international manufacturing fair to discover innovations in the industry and learn how Materialise powers additive manufacturing (AM) champions. Meet us and we’ll show you how to get the best out of CAD and mesh during file and build preparation with Magics 26. Our experts will be available and look forward to helping you reach your 3D printing goals with our end-to-end software platform.

Book a spot

* Please note: It is not possible to register for MECSPE on this page. Please visit the official site to purchase tickets or complete your registration.

What you will learn

  • Gain insights on how to get the best out of CAD and mesh for file and build preparation using Magics 26
  • Learn how our AM-native enterprise platform enables repeatability and greater connectivity in your production
  • Discover how we’re partnering with others to drive solutions and deliver meaningful 3DP applications
  • View demos on Magics 26, Link3D, Build Processor, Workflow Automation, Process Tuner, and Storefront software
